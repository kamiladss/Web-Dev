Dutch Apple Pie

  • Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Apples 6 large
Sugar 1 cup
Cinnamon 1 teaspoon
Pie Crust 1 pre-made or homemade

  • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  • Step 2: Peel and slice the apples. Toss them with sugar and cinnamon.

  • Step 3: Fill the pie crust with the apple mixture.

  • Step 4: Cover with a lattice crust and bake for 45-50 minutes until golden brown.

French Fries

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 4 large
Vegetable Oil Enough for deep frying
Salt To taste

  • Step 1: Peel and slice the potatoes into thin strips.

  • Step 2: Soak the potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.

  • Step 3: Heat oil in a deep fryer or pot to 180°C.

  • Step 4: Fry the potatoes in batches until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.

Omelette

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4 large
Milk 2 tablespoons
Butter 1 tablespoon
Salt and Pepper To taste

  • Step 1: Whisk the eggs with milk, salt, and pepper.

  • Step 2: Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.

  • Step 3: Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it cook for 2-3 minutes.

  • Step 4: Fold the omelette in half and serve warm.
